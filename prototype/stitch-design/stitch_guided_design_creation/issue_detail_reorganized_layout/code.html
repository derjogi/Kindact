Proposal Details

The comprehensive network expansion aims to bridge existing gaps between the northern commercial district and the southern residential belt.

1. Infrastructure Specifications

Standardized 2.5m width lanes with concrete curbs for physical separation. Source #4 indicates this reduces collision risk by 40%. The choice of non-permeable surfacing was selected for durability in extreme seasonal shifts.

2. Urban Integration

Integration will require removal of 14 non-metered parking spots along 5th Ave. The trade-off has been identified as a key point of friction in preliminary ward meetings.
