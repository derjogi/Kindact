Design details

  • Dotted underline on referenced phrases in idle state — subtle hint that they're interactive
  • Hover highlights the phrase (warm tint) and dims the rest of the summary — focuses attention
  • Side panel animates in the source cards (smooth transition, no layout shift)
  • Click on a source card scrolls to that comment and briefly highlights it
  • Mobile: tap on dotted phrase → bottom sheet slides up. Swipe down to dismiss.
  • Unreferenced text: no dotted underline, no interaction — honest about what's traced and what isn't
